User's Guide
===============================

Fire Insights makes it incredibly fast and easy to do Self-Serve Data Preparation and Advanced Analytics. 
With the power of Fire Insights at your hands you can seamlessly find value from your data and scale to Petabytes of data. 
You can install it on the cloud, on-premise or even on your laptop. Fire Insights integrates seamlessly with the complex of Enterprise Environments.
